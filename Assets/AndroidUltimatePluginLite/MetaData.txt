an  Android Plugin for Unity3d that will help you access features on Android devices with in unity3d.<br><br>

This plugin will speeds up your work and saves a lot of time compare on creating your own plugin which requires learning, researching, coding and testing on how to make things work.<br><br>

<STRONG>Note:</STRONG> this is a Lite Version of Android Ultimate Plugin for you to test, before buying the paid version.<br><br>

<STRONG>Paid version has more features that's why we recommend you to buy it.</STRONG><br><br>

<STRONG>List of Features:</STRONG><br><br>

1. Local Notification<br>
2. Screenshot and Share Image<br>
3. Share Text or URL<br>
4. Immersive Full Screen Mode<br>
5.Native Android UI Loading<br>
6. Native Android Rate US Popup<br>
7. Native Android Alert Popup<br>
8. Native Android Toast Message<br><br>

<STRONG>Note: Does not extend UnityPlayerActivity</STRONG><br><br>
